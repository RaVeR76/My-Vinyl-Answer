My Vinyl Answer .... What is it all about?


Any vinyl collector knows the pain of going through their collection every once in a while and trying to figure out what genre is what or what year a tune is? You try with your wee pieces of paper or card to seperate certain groups of vinyl apart but these don't last very long ... and then it's back to having a whole bunch of vinyl again.

Well have no fear cause My Vinyl Anwser is here !

It will keep track of your very personal vinyl collection where you can create, read, update and delete your way to vinyl heaven. Slide through your vinyl collection wherever or whenever you want and don't forget to check your collection before you buy another copy of that awesome tune that you've already bought 3 times.

Anyways, I hope you like it ..... peace out !
